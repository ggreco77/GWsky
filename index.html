Welcome to GitHub Pages.

The interactive script GWsky (v2) defines a sequence of Fields of View (FoV) centers from a fixed position over the sky. North/South/East/West directions are allowed. The results are displayed in Aladin Sky Atlas (http://aladin.u-strasbg.fr/) using the |SAMPIntegratedClient| class. The airmass at the FoV center and the integrated probability (%) are provided during the FoV sequence.

The FoV sequence is generated along the coordinate grid; no roll angle is provided in this release. The FoV centers were evenly space assuming that the shortest angular distance between two points on the celestial sphere is measured along a great circle that passes through both of them:

                        cosθ=sinδ1sinδ2+cosδ1cosδ2cos(α1−α2),

where (α1,δ1) and (α2,δ2) are the right ascension and declination of the two points on the sky (expressed in degrees, then converted to radians).
